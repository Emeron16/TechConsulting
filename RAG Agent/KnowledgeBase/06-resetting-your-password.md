# Resetting Your Password

TechConsulting passwords expire every 90 days and must be at least 14 characters, including one number and one symbol. You can reset your password yourself at any time using self-service password reset (SSPR) — you do not need to contact IT for a routine reset.

## Steps — self-service reset (you remember your current password)

1. Press **Ctrl+Alt+Delete** on your work computer and choose **Change a password**.
2. Enter your current password, then your new password twice.
3. Your new password syncs to all TechConsulting systems (email, VPN, Wi-Fi, file servers) within about 5 minutes.

## Steps — forgotten password (self-service portal)

1. From any browser, go to **reset.techconsulting.com**.
2. Enter your work email address.
3. Verify your identity via the MFA method you enrolled (Microsoft Authenticator push or a registered phone number).
4. Choose a new password meeting the complexity requirements above.

## If self-service reset fails

If you're unable to complete identity verification (for example, you lost the device your MFA is enrolled on), self-service reset will not work and this requires manual identity verification by IT Security — submit a ticket through the TC Helpdesk Portal or call the helpdesk directly at extension 4357, since this cannot be completed over email for security reasons.
