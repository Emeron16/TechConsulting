# Connecting to Corporate Wi-Fi

TechConsulting offices broadcast two wireless networks: **TC-Corporate** (WPA2-Enterprise, for company-managed devices) and **TC-Guest** (isolated internet-only access, for visitors and personal devices).

## Connecting to TC-Corporate (company laptops)

1. Click the Wi-Fi icon in the system tray.
2. Select **TC-Corporate** from the list of networks.
3. When prompted, enter your TechConsulting network username in the format `techconsulting\firstname.lastname` and your standard password.
4. Accept the certificate prompt if one appears — this confirms you're connecting to the genuine TechConsulting network, not an imposter access point.
5. Your device will remember this network and reconnect automatically going forward.

## Connecting to TC-Guest (personal devices, visitors)

1. Select **TC-Guest** from the Wi-Fi list.
2. Open a browser; you'll be redirected to a guest portal.
3. Enter your name and the name of the TechConsulting employee you're visiting, or a guest code provided by the front desk.
4. Accept the acceptable use terms to get internet access. Note that TC-Guest cannot reach internal file servers, printers, or applications — it is internet-only.

## Troubleshooting

If TC-Corporate repeatedly fails to authenticate, your account password may have expired — try a password reset first (see "Resetting Your Password"). If the network doesn't appear at all, you may be out of range of an access point; contact your office manager about coverage in your area.
