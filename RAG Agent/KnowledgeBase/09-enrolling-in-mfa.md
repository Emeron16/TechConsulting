# Enrolling in Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

All TechConsulting accounts require multi-factor authentication (MFA) using the Microsoft Authenticator app. MFA is required to sign in to email, VPN, and most internal applications, and is mandatory for every employee within 7 days of their start date.

## Steps

1. Install **Microsoft Authenticator** from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) on your personal or company mobile phone.
2. On your computer, go to **myaccount.microsoft.com** and sign in with your work email and password.
3. Click **Security info**, then **Add sign-in method**, and choose **Authenticator app**.
4. Follow the on-screen QR code — open Microsoft Authenticator on your phone, tap the **+** icon, choose **Work or school account**, and scan the code.
5. Approve the test notification sent to your phone to confirm enrollment.
6. Optionally, add a backup method (a registered phone number for SMS codes) in case you lose access to your primary device.

## Notes

If you get a new phone, you must re-enroll MFA before your old device's Authenticator stops working — do this in advance by installing Authenticator on the new phone and adding it as an additional method before removing the old one. If you're fully locked out with no working MFA device, this cannot be self-serviced; contact IT Security directly through the TC Helpdesk Portal or by phone, since re-enrollment after a full lockout requires manual identity verification.
