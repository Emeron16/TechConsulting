# Setting Up Your Email Client (Outlook)

TechConsulting uses Microsoft 365 for email. Outlook comes pre-configured on company-issued devices, but you may need to set it up manually after a device reimage, on a new device, or when adding your work email to a personal computer.

## Steps (Outlook desktop, company device)

1. Open **Outlook**.
2. If this is the first launch, it should auto-detect your account from your Windows sign-in. Click **Next** through the setup wizard.
3. If prompted for credentials, enter your work email address (`[email protected]`) and your standard network password.
4. Approve the MFA prompt on your Microsoft Authenticator app if requested.
5. Outlook will sync your mailbox — this can take several minutes for large mailboxes on first setup.

## Steps (personal device or mobile)

Personal devices require the **Outlook mobile app** or **Outlook on the web** (outlook.office.com) — TechConsulting does not permit configuring work email in third-party mail apps or the native iOS/Android mail apps, since these don't enforce our data-loss-prevention policies. Sign in with your work email and password, and complete MFA when prompted. Note that connecting email to a personal device will enroll that device in basic mobile device management (screen lock and remote wipe of the work profile only — personal data is not affected).

## Troubleshooting

If Outlook gets stuck on "Trying to connect," confirm you have internet access and that you're not blocked by an outdated password (see "Resetting Your Password"). If your mailbox doesn't sync fully, allow up to 24 hours for very large mailboxes, or contact IT if it stalls completely.
